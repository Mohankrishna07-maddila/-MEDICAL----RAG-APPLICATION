Frequently Asked Questions – ABC Health Insurance

Q: How do I check my policy coverage?
A: Policy coverage details are available in the policy document or via the mobile app.

Q: What is a cashless claim?
A: A cashless claim allows treatment at a network hospital without paying upfront, subject to approval.

Q: How long does claim settlement take?
A: Cashless claims are usually settled within 2–4 hours.
Reimbursement claims take 7–14 working days after document submission.

Q: What documents are required for reimbursement?
A:
- Discharge summary
- Original bills
- Prescription copies
- Claim form
