1. SCOPE OF COVER
The policy covers hospitalization expenses including room rent, doctor fees, and prescribed medicines. Claims must be filed within 30 days of discharge. Coverage is subject to the receipt of premium and information provided in the Proposal or Information Summary Sheet.

2. CORE BENEFITS
2.1 In-patient Treatment: Covers Doctorsâ€™ fees, Diagnostics Tests, Medicines, Operation theatre charges, ICU charges, and internal prosthetics.

2.2 Hospital Accommodation: Covers reasonable and customary charges for room stay.

2.3 Pre-hospitalization: Covers medical expenses incurred up to 30 days before admission (reimbursement only).

2.4 Post-hospitalization: Covers medical expenses incurred up to 60 days after discharge (reimbursement only).

2.5 Day-Care Procedures: Covers medical procedures lasting less than 24 hours (e.g., Dialysis, Chemotherapy) that normally require hospitalization.

2.6 Domiciliary Treatment: Treatment at home is covered if it exceeds 3 days and the patient cannot be moved to a hospital or a bed is unavailable.

2.7 Organ Donor: Covers harvesting expenses if the organ is for the Insured Person.

2.8 Emergency Ambulance: Coverage limited to Rs. 2,000 per event for Out-of-Network hospitals.

3. COST SHARING (CO-PAY)
Insured persons aged 65 or over must bear 20% of the admissible claim amount (80% paid by the Insurer).

4. WAITING PERIODS & EXCLUSIONS
4.a Pre-Existing Conditions: 48-month waiting period from the first policy inception.

4.b Initial Waiting Period: 30 days from policy start (except for accidents).

4.c Specific Waiting Period (24 Months): Includes stones, cysts, cataracts, hernia, diabetes complications, and joint replacements.

Permanent Exclusions: * Addictive disorders (alcohol/substance abuse).

Cosmetic surgery (unless due to cancer or burns).

Hereditary conditions and HIV/AIDS.

Non-allopathic treatments (Ayurveda, Homeopathy, etc.).

Self-inflicted injuries or suicide attempts.

Treatment received outside India.
